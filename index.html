Simple answers to the questions owners actually ask

Honest, compact answers on fit, control, seasonality, and how the service works.

What exactly do you manage?

PrimeVlore focuses on the key parts that affect rental performance and owner experience: pricing, listing quality, guest communication, turnover coordination, issue escalation, and owner reporting.

Do I still keep control as the owner?

Yes. The goal is not to remove owner visibility. The goal is to give you a stronger local execution layer while keeping approvals, communication, and reporting clear.

Do you work with Airbnb and Booking.com?

Yes. PrimeVlore is positioned around short-term rental performance across the main booking platforms, including Airbnb and Booking.com.

Do you offer partial-service or flexible models?

Yes. Not every apartment or owner needs the same support model all year. The structure can be lighter or more hands-on depending on the season, apartment, and owner preference.

What happens in winter or slower months?

Winter strategy should not be an afterthought. Depending on the apartment, that may mean lighter support, longer stays, different pricing posture, or a selective low-season operating plan.

Is every apartment a fit?

No. Some apartments have stronger short-term rental potential than others. The audit gives a clear view of fit, issues, and likely upside before moving further.

What does PrimeVlore charge?

Pricing is based on service scope and property setup. There is no public standard rate because support can be lighter or more involved depending on season and owner goals. Pricing is discussed directly in the audit conversation — with no obligation.
